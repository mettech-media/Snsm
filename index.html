About Us

SNSM Computer & Technical Education Center, located in Old Santoshnagar under the aegis of Bhagyanagar, Old City welfare organization. A Non-Profitable Service Organization established in 1991, August 215 working for Major of Technical Courses. The institute very well stands out with its sole purpose of upliftment of weaker sections, economically backward class (EBC) and below poverty line communities irrespective of caste and creed. We successfully have transformed and helped 12,000 candidates over the years, since the inception 1991. They have provided placements locally as well as abroad.

Founder

The founder of the institution was late Munawar Ali, a retired Ex-Servicemen, Indian Army. Before the independence of India, i.e. in the year 1947, after the World War I (WWII) was over (1945), the British were ruling the nation. They sent the Indians who worked in Indian Peace Keeping forces to Afghanistan, Egypt, Cyprus, etc. Mr. Munawar was also accepted by the troops and he was there for 3- 4 years. After returning back to India, he retired and rendered his entire life for the society. He passed away at the age of 97, in 2013. In the year 1991, he had established a society named M/S Bhagyanagar Old City welfare organization, in Hyderabad. His objective was to make the educated unemployed youth to be the bread winners for their families.
